🎨 Drawing Playground

Pick a theme, and drawings come to life on the big screen in real time

Drawing Playground is a free real-time collaborative drawing wall for parties, classrooms, and events. Pick a theme, share the QR code, and anyone nearby can draw on their phone or tablet — their drawing floats onto the big screen instantly. No sign-up, no app install, and nothing is saved after the playground ends.

How it works

1

Pick a theme

Ocean, sky, forest, space, party, jungle, winter — or upload your own photo as the background.

2

Share the QR code

Anyone nearby scans it with their phone or tablet camera. No app to install, no account to make.

3

Watch it come alive

Drawings and messages float onto the big screen in real time as people send them in.

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes. There's no cost, no account, and no watermark. Just pick a theme and start.

Do people need to install an app to draw?

No. Scanning the QR code opens a drawing screen directly in their phone or tablet's browser.

Is anything saved after the playground ends?

By default, nothing is stored on our servers — drawings only exist in memory while the session is active and disappear when it ends. The host can optionally save a photo of the wall to their own device before ending the session; that happens entirely in their browser and isn't uploaded anywhere.

Can I use my own photo as the background?

Yes — choose the "Custom Photo" option when picking a theme and upload any image from your device.

How many people can draw at once?

As many as want to scan the QR code. The big screen keeps the most recent drawings floating and gently retires older ones to make room for new ones.
