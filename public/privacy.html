Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-18

Drawing Playground ("the Service") is a free, real-time collaborative drawing tool. This page explains what data is collected when you use it and how it is handled.

What we collect

  • Drawings and optional names you submit are transmitted to the host's screen in real time. They are kept only in server memory for the lifetime of the playground session (up to 12 hours, or until the host ends it) and are never written to disk or a database.
  • No accounts, no sign-up. We do not collect email addresses, passwords, or any account information — the Service does not have user accounts.
  • Standard server logs (such as IP address and request timing) may be recorded temporarily for rate-limiting and abuse prevention.

Advertising and cookies

Some pages of this Service display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data and manage your ad preferences at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Data retention

Drawings and session data exist only while a playground session is active and are permanently discarded when the session ends or expires. We do not retain drawings or names after that point.

Children's privacy

The Service does not require any personal information to participate — no name, email, or account is required, and the optional name field is not verified or linked to any identity. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is voluntarily and optionally entered into the drawing screen.
